Our attractions

Cape Fear

Cape Fear screams from a 30-foot-high platform through a darkened tunnel for a length of 215 feet, using single or double inflated tubes.

Fun-L-Tunnel

The Fun-L-Tunnel is a slide on the wild side. This narrow darkened tunnel drops 30 feet without a tube.

Zoom Flume

It descends from the same 30-foot-high tower as the Cape Fear and Fun-L-Tunnel and goes on for 200 feet.

Drop-Slides

The Drop Slides offer a thrill factor by giving guests a similar feeling to free falling with nothing in the way to slow you down.

Ecological Boardwalk

Located along the shores of beautiful Lake Kennedy, the Ecological Boardwalk is a terrific place to take a walk and enjoy nature.

Family Pool

Whether you float in from the Main Stream River or take advantage of our zero-depth entrance, the Family Pool is a great place to cool off and have fun.

Pro Racers

These child-sized slides are not as scary as the big rides giving little ones a chance to enjoy thrills made especially for them

Electric Slide

The Electric Slide drops five stories, twisting and turning for 457 feet, sending riders on a 27-second plunge into its own pool.

Thunder Bump

The longest of three, high-speed slides and features several steep bumps that give guests the feeling of going airborne.
