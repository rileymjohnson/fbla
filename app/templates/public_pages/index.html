Welcome!

Welcome to Five Stars Family Entertainment Center! We are an amusement park local to Houston, Texas that is geared towards great fun for the whole family. We guarantee a wonderful time for customers of all ages! Spend a day winning fantastic prizes in our world class arcade, go for a thrilling ride on one of our exciting roller coasters, or relax in our exotic Five Star Lounge. We’re all about your enjoyment here at Five Stars, and every one of our employees is hand picked to help you have the best time you can. The fun never stops at Five Stars, and we can’t wait for you to stop by and have the time of your life!
