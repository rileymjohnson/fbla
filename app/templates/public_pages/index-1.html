About Vanilla

Located in the heart of Orlando and celebrating 125 years of fun, Vanilla is America’s only Downtown Theme and Water Park. The park is open May 2nd through November 1st.

There’s something for everyone, with 53 rides and attractions, including 14 pint-sized adventures, thrilling roller coasters and an entire water park. This year, there’s more entertainment than ever before with exciting new family shows Conjure and Disney’s Octonauts, Dive-In Movies, a free summer concert series and three stunning fireworks extravaganza that will light up the Orlando skyline, plus Fright Fest in October!
And don’t miss the breathtaking views of Orlando atop the 20-story Observation Tower or while plummeting 200 feet on the Tower of Doom.
